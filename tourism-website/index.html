Cultural Heritage

Discover the rich cultural traditions of Odisha

Odissi Dance

One of the oldest classical dance forms of India, characterized by sensuousness and lyricism.

Pattachitra Painting

Traditional cloth-based scroll painting with intricate details and vibrant colors.

Pipili Applique Work

Colorful patchwork textile that showcases the artistic heritage of Odisha.

Sambalpuri Sarees

Traditional handloom sarees known for their unique ikat patterns and designs.

Local Cuisine

Savor the authentic flavors of Odisha

Dalma

A traditional dish made with lentils and vegetables, served with rice.

Chhena Poda

A sweet delicacy made from cottage cheese, sugar, and cardamom.

Pakhala Bhata

Fermented rice with water, served with fried fish, vegetables, and curd.

Rasagola

Soft, spongy cheese balls soaked in sugar syrup, a famous Odia sweet.
